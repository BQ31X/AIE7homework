With the conclusion of the federal student loan repayment pause, borrowers can utilize IDR plans that cap monthly payments at a percentage of their discretionary income. Additionally, efforts have been made to simplify the enrollment process and reduce the frequency of annual income recertification, enhancing the overall experience for borrowers.

It's also crucial for borrowers to be aware of the various forgiveness options available, such as the Public Service Loan Forgiveness program. This program allows eligible borrowers to have their student loans discharged after a designated number of qualifying payments.
